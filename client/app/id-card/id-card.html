Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

It takes 7 to 10 business days to receive an ID card once it has been mailed. Dental cards are only available for printing and will not be mailed. However, you don't need to show a card to get dental care.

If there is incorrect information on your member ID card, and you signed up for insurance through your workplace, your benefit administrator can give us the name change for you. If you did not sign up through you workplace, please call the member services number on the back of the card.
